I've been working on something big for a while now, and it's time to share it.

It's called [Vemgram](https://vemgram.com/) – a platform that connects manufacturers across India with the buyers who need them.

India has thousands of factories doing incredible work. But most of them are hard to find, hard to verify, and hard to trust – especially if you're a retailer, exporter, or bulk buyer looking for the right supplier. Vemgram fixes that. Every manufacturer on the platform has a proper profile with verified details, product listings, and a direct way to get in touch.

For manufacturers, it means real visibility. For buyers, it means finding the right factory without the usual guesswork.

We're live with Solar and Textiles right now, and more industries are on the way.

There's a lot that goes into building something like this – research, strategy, understanding the market, making sure everything we put out actually works for the people using it. It's been one of the most rewarding things I've worked on.

Go explore at vemgram.com – and if you're a manufacturer or know one, we'd love to have you on board.

Building this alongside my friend [Deepak](https://deepakness.com/) has made the journey even better.
