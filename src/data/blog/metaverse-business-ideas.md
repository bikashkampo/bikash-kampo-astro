### Aerospace industry

#### Aircraft design studios

Aerospace companies and design teams could create virtual aircraft design studios within the metaverse, allowing them to collaborate and work on projects in a fully immersive and interactive environment. These studios could be equipped with virtual tools and resources for creating and visualizing aircraft designs, and could allow team members to work together in real-time from anywhere in the world.

#### Pilot training

Aviation companies could offer virtual pilot training programs within the metaverse, allowing pilots to learn and practice flying skills in a fully immersive and interactive environment. These training programs could include virtual simulations of different aircraft and flying scenarios, and could be accessed by pilots from anywhere in the world.

#### Space tourism

In the future, space tourism may become more common. Businesses could create virtual space tourism experiences in the metaverse, allowing people to experience what it’s like to be an astronaut without the need for physical travel.

#### Space debris tracking and removal

The metaverse could be used to track and remove space debris, allowing companies to develop and test new technologies and techniques in a virtual environment before implementing them in the real world.

#### Aircraft manufacturing

The metaverse could be used to streamline the aircraft manufacturing process, allowing companies to design, prototype, and test new aircraft in a virtual environment before building physical prototypes.
