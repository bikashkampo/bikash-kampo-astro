I am a firm believer in small bets.

And nowadays, I am all in for small bets like Programmatic SEO OS.

Can you imagine building a massive info product with 500+ manually curated resources and ideas, 10,000+ words of text, and more than 5 hours of recorded video tutorials in just 18 days?

I could have easily been trapped in the endless procrastination loop before launching the OS. But glad that [Deepak](https://twitter.com/DeepakNesss) and I set a deadline and met the deadline on time, no matter how much effort it needed.

If you’re wondering, here’s the complete story.

On December 18th, 2022, Deepak and I were talking about doing a small fun project that doesn’t take a lot of our time. Within 1–2 days, we finalized that we will be creating the “[Programmatic SEO OS](https://pseoos.com)” and launching it on Jan 5th, 2023 – in just 18 days.

It was a challenging commitment as we both had to work for more than 10 hours every day for 18 straight days, but we stick to it. I took on all the resource collection and organization parts, and Deepak took on the writing and video-making parts.

And it makes me so happy that we didn’t give up.

I remember that our only goal was to make at least $1000 from the OS that we were about to launch. But at the time of writing this, we have already made more than $5000 from this.

We are getting some great feedback from our customers as well.

![](https://pub-bfd70a093bc24400bb25f969aed67b0f.r2.dev/blog/image.png)

In the future, I will be experimenting with several small and low-cost projects that I can gather valuable feedback from. And if I get good results, I can double down and turn them into more ambitious projects.

Cheers! 🥂
