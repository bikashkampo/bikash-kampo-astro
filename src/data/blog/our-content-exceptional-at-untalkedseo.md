A while ago, in April 2022, Deepak and I jumped into the world of programmatic SEO. There weren’t many people talking about it then. We had to figure out a lot by ourselves. But today the buzz around programmatic SEO has grown a lot.

Today seeing this Buzz any marketing-related company or website start writing a blog on Programmatic SEO. Everybody just writes a couple of articles and that to a guide on pSEO just with the hope of ranking and selling their own product or service.

The quality was not that great. We were not happy.

At UntalkedSEO we always believed it’s not just about the quantity of information.

Quality matters!

## Why were we not happy with other pSEO content?

As a team of SEO practitioners, we weren’t satisfied with the available resources. The majority of content in our field isn’t high quality and most of them are not covered.

![](https://pub-bfd70a093bc24400bb25f969aed67b0f.r2.dev/blog/image.png)

Most content out there was aimed at search engines, not people like you and me.

Many experts were too busy to create good quality content. And it was hard to find good content among the many things written.

So, we decided to do something about it.

[UntalkedSEO](https://untalkedseo.com/about/) -> A content site devoted to programmatic SEO knowledge and resources.

At UntalkedSEO, we don’t just follow what everyone else is doing. We believe in learning by doing. We try out ideas, see what works and what doesn’t, and we share these experiences with you.

Our method is simple:

-   Ask a lot of questions
-   brainstorm ideas
-   create a plan
-   test it out
-   look at what we learned
-   repeat the process with new ideas

We focus on what’s important to you and me. In this way, we’ve made UntalkedSEO a go-to source for anyone interested in programmatic SEO.

We don’t chase trends; we believe in sharing reliable, well-researched, and useful content.

Stick with us, and let’s keep learning about pSEO together, one step at a time.
